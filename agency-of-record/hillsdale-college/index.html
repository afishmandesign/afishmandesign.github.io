Hillsdale College
Challenge: Make Hillsdale a premier national choice among high achieving high school students. Solution: Revisit the institution’s brand from the ground up from clarifying core mission and messaging with visual repositioning appealing to more contemporary look and feel. Hillsdale is a small, unique liberal arts college providing commanding national thought leadership in the Classics, Western civilization and Governance. Lead designer on the account, developing master brand look and feel, photography style, setting design standards across print and web, implementing print strategies on numerous accounts for Undergraduate Admissions, Graduate, Alumni and K-12 audiences. A 4-year account.
