The Chicago Community Trust

AAt the beginning of the Covid-19 crisis, The Trust provided large sums of matching funds to provide relief to Chicago citizens through partnerships with United Way and other corporations. As a result of these efforts, The Trust created a Chicago metropolitan regional initiative called Together We Rise to explore new ways of financial engagement with Chiago businesses and endeavors towards a new social contract. Presentation features commissioned photography done by local photographers specifically for this project. The introduction to this public initiative was kicked off by this presentation. Accompanying voice-over led to discussion.
